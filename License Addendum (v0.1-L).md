License Addendum (v0.1-L)

This addendum may be attached to any license, terms of service, or repository.

1. Claim of Compliance

Any claim that a system is “Freedom & Accountability–Compliant” requires verifiable adherence to:

the core standard

the enforcement spec

the compliance checklist

2. Burden of Proof

The burden of proof lies with the system owner, not the user.

Opacity invalidates compliance claims.

3. Revocation

If a system is found to violate the standard:

the compliance claim must be removed

or the violation must be corrected and documented

4. No Partial Branding

The name or branding of this standard may not be used to imply partial or aspirational compliance.

Either the system complies, or it does not.

5. Forking & Modification

Forks may adapt this standard only if:

deviations are documented

the fork is clearly renamed

original principles are not misrepresented

Enforcement Reality (Read This Twice)

This standard does not grant trust.
It enables verification.

If verification is impossible, compliance is false.
